英语语法

04 / 12 / 2023 / 木

五大句型
主语 + [不及物]动词:
  subject + intransitive verb

主语 + [单及物]动词 + 宾语:
  subject + [mono]transitive verb + object

主语 + [双及物]动词 + 间接宾语 + 直接宾语:
  subject + ditransitive verb + indirect object + direct object

主语 + [复杂及物]动词 + 宾语 + 宾语补语
  subject + complex-transitive verb + object + [object] complement

主语 + [连/系]动词 + 主语补语/表语
  subject + linking verb + [subject] complement/predicate
句子结构
简单句: simple sentence
复合句/并列句: compound sentence
复杂句[主/从句]: complex sentence [main clause/subordinate clause]
句子成分
主语: subject
谓语动词: predicate verb
宾语: object
宾语补语: object complement
主语补语: predicate
定语: attribute
状语: adverbial
同位语: appositive
词性/词类
动词: verbs
名词: nouns
冠词: articles
代词: pronouns
形容词: adjectives
数词: numerals
副词: adverbs
介词: prepositions
叹词: interjections
连词: conjunctions

  及物动词: transitive verbs

  连系动词

疑问句: 助动词/疑问副词
一般疑问句: 是否 Yes or No
Did the rat eat the rice?

特殊疑问句:
What did the rat eat?

选择疑问句:
Did the rat eat a cake or a rice?

反义疑问句:
The rat ate the rice, didn't it?
动词时态: 时间+状态
时间: 过去/过去将来/现在/将来
状态: 一般/进行/完成/完成进行
英语表达将来:
  一般现在时: The bus leaves at 8PM.
  be [going] to:
    I'm going to take a bus.
    You are to take a bus.
  be about to:
    I'm about to take a bus.
将来时: will/shall + 动词原形
过去将来时: would/should + 动词原形

过去时: 过去式

现在进行时: be + 现在分词
现在完成时: have + 过去分词
现在完成进行时: have been + 现在分词

将来完成时: will have + 过去分词

过去将来完成时: would have + 过去分词
动词语气
陈述/祈使[命令,请求]/虚拟/疑问/条件/感叹
虚拟语气
与事实相反的假设:
  与现在相反: if + 过去式; would + 动词原型
  与过去相反: if + 过去完成; would have + 过去分词
  与将来相反: if + should + 动词原型; would + 动词原形
  If I should meet thee long years, how should I greet thee.

主观愿望: 愿望,请求,建议,命令,等
  wish/would rather…
    I wish [that] I were there.
    I wish I had been a better man.
    I wish I could/should/might/would be a better man.
    I would rather that you hadn't read too many books.
  demand/suggest/insist/order
    I demand that you do it.
    I demand[ed] that you [should] do it.
    It's suggested that you [should] be a better man.
非谓语动词: non-finitive verbs
主语/宾语/补语/定语/状语

动词不定式: infinitive
  full-infinitive: 完全不定式
    To be or not to be, that's the question.
  bare-infinitive: 裸不定式
    You help me [to] make a cake.
  动词短语:
    look forward to[prep.] + doing

动名词: 动词原形+ing
    Seeing is believing.
    The rats stop to eat rice.
    The rats stop eating rice.

现在分词: 形容词性,修饰[主动]状态
  The news is interesting.
  I see a rat eating rice.

过去分词: 形容词性,修饰[被动]状态
  The rat is interested in eating rice.
  The rice is interesting to the rats.
  The work finished, the rat went home.
独立主格结构
名词+非谓语动词/being
Because she's invited by the Queen, the girl decided to go to the party.
=>
Invited by the Queen, the girl decided to go to the party.

Because the Queen invited her, the girl decided to go to the party.
=>
The Queen inviting her, the girl decided to go to the party.

If the weather permits, the rat will go out.
=>
The weather permitting, the rat will go out.

After the work was finished, the rat went home.
=>
The work finished, the rat went home.

宾语从句:
  that 口语或非正式书面语可以省略
    I know [that] you are smart.

  主从句时态同步: 客观规律除外
    I knew [that] the sun rises in the east.
    I thought [that] you were smart.

  be + 形容词 + that: 宾语/同位语/状语？
    I'm afraid that you are not smart.

  否定: 谓语动词前,不要放在从句中
    I didn't think you were smart.
